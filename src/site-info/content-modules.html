Module Rules / Guidelines

* in desktop mode, grids should have a single row. can switch to two rows in tablet or mobile as needed.
* only .content-containers or images should sit on the grid (ie be assigned grid column coordinates). everything else (eg, content items) should be inside of a .content-container.

Content Items
* all content items should go inside of a .content-container DIV
* any vertical margin between siblings should be placed on the sibling above

Income with dignity  

In the face of inevitable economic upheaval, Income Movement champions a basic income as a means to a healthier, more innovative, and just society.

We believe that the economy is broken. Currently, many of the people who work hardest and are the backbones of a strong economy are those who are most vulnerable to financial hardship and insecurity. It is time to shift our thinking and recognize that we can design a system that works for everyone.

Congressional Pressure

Our goal is to get federal basic income legislation passed by 2030. Income Movement leads the basic income grassroots community in applying pressure to elected officials to support direct cash programs in the fight to end poverty. From monthly stimulus checks, to expanded Child Tax Credit permanency and more, we provide the tools, actions and events for people to demand leadership support a guaranteed basic income.

PILLAR 1

Grassroots Organizing

Organize the most passionate basic income supporters to help lead this movement at the local, regional and national level. Create a strategic roadmap with targeted initiatives that provide opportunities for people to work towards larger goals by taking action, promoting basic income, and demonstrating publicly.

PILLAR 2

Thought Leadership

Bring together social justice and basic income organizations, experts and influencers to guide the larger movement towards strategic outcomes. Identify key language and tools for educating the public on basic income, develop a shared roadmap to minimize redundancies and coordinate across initiatives and events to maximize cross organization support.

PILLAR 3

Political impact

Establish basic income as a legitimate political issue by defining the support base and highlighting their importance as voters. Track direct cash programs and basic income legislation and connect grassroots and organizations to opportunities to advocate and persuade those in power to adopt pro-basic income stances.

TOGETHER WE CAN

Our mission

The energy surrounding basic income and economic solutions for a healthy 21st Century America is stronger than ever. Income Movement started with a worldwide spanning 30 cities, in 13 countries, on six continents. 10,000 people showed up to demand change. This is just the beginning.

Our country is experiencing massive upheaval, even before COVID. 43 million Americans live in poverty. 37% of American adults don’t work. 73 million American jobs will be automated within 10 years. In the land of ‘equal opportunity’ none of our solutions have leveled the playing field.
The solution is to give cash to as many people as possible and empower them to do what makes most sense to their needs. No one deserves to start at zero. An equal head start means better outcomes for all.

Income Movement is made up of former tech entrepreneurs, retail workers, students, political organizers, social workers, union members, and artists. We are former campaign volunteers, social justice organizers, Yang 2020 staffers, and long-term Basic Income advocates. We are proud members of our communities, dedicated to making things better for all. And we are everyone who joins us - which includes you!
