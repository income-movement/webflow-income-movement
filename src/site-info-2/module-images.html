Image Pixel Dimensions

For Content Providers – these are the basic image sizes for the various aspect ratios used on the site. For each aspect ratio, there are values for Small, Medium and Large images.

The use of different images sizes is one of the easiest ways to improve the user experience by reducing the amount of downloaded data. It is especially important/thoughtful to people on mobile to minimize the amount of data they use to look at our site over a slower connection.

The dimensions listed below are for horizontal images. For vertical images, simply reverse the values (400x300 >> 300x400)
