Change the system

Income with dignity  

In the face of inevitable economic upheaval, Income Movement champions a basic income as a means to a healthier, more innovative, and just society.

We believe that the economy is broken. Currently, many of the people who work hardest and are the backbones of a strong economy are those who are most vulnerable to financial hardship and insecurity. It is time to shift our thinking and recognize that we can design a system that works for everyone.

Our Initiatives

Basic Income March

On September 25th, 2021 we took to the streets for the 3rd Annual Basic Income March, to demand that our leadership adopt and pass a federal basic income. From Jakarta, Indonesia to Denver, Colorado, people around the globe came together in solidarity on this day to celebrate together the progress we are making towards basic income and to tell elected officials that we will will not stop until our economy works for the people.

100 Mayors Campaign

The fight for guaranteed income must happen at all levels of government. Grassroots can have a huge impact on local leadership, who can in turn influence elected officials at the state and federal level. From this truth, the 100 Mayors for Guaranteed Income Campaign is born. The campaign is designed for constituents across the country to petition and call their Mayors, urging them to support guaranteed income. By the end of 2021, we will reach 100 Mayors endorsing Guaranteed Income.

NABIG Congress

The North American Basic Income Guarantee Congress is an annual Conference focused on exploring ideas, hearing from experts and organizers, and charting the course for basic income legislation. Hosted by Basic income Canada, US Basic Income Guarantee, Income Movement and leaders across the community, the 2021 Congress is a three day virtual event that will highlight pilots, legislation, grassroots growth and more. Congressional Pressure

Our goal is to get federal basic income legislation passed by 2030. Income Movement leads the basic income grassroots community in applying pressure to elected officials to support direct cash programs in the fight to end poverty. From monthly stimulus checks, to expanded Child Tax Credit permanency and more, we provide the tools, actions and events for people to demand leadership support a guaranteed basic income.
