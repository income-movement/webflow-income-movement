Why
Income Movement?

Our experience shows us that real change is complicated. Movements are built by people and protests, coalitions, viral videos, committed volunteers and lawmakers. Income Movement is here to help organize it all.

Our mission

Income Movement is working to move basic income from a radical idea to widespread public acceptance and organize a grassroots movement to make basic income our reality in the next decade.

Organize together

We are an inclusive, people-powered movement on a mission to build an economy that works for everyone. The annual Basic Income March is our community's way of marking the growth of our movement. We marched, we rallied, and took action! Learn more about this incredible day of action.
