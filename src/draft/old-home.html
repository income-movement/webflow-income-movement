Why
Income Movement?

Our experience shows us that real change is complicated. Movements are built by people and protests, money, followers, and viral videos, committed volunteers and lawmakers. Income Movement is here to help organize it all.

join today

Our mission

Income Movement is a people-powered movement to implement basic income. In the face of inevitable economic upheaval, we champion a trickle up economy as a means to a healthier, more innovative, and just society.
